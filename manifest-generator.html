How to use this page
1
Evaluate your items

Use the Decision Tree app to go through your belongings and sort each one. Anything you plan to ship to Honduras should be marked Keep. The more complete your list, the more accurate your manifest will be.

2
Export your data from the Decision Tree

In the Decision Tree app, tap the Data tab in the bottom navigation, then tap Export Backup (JSON). A file named Honduras_Manifest_Backup.json will download to your device. This file contains your complete item log.

3
Load the file on this page

Tap the upload area in Step 1 above and select your JSON file. The page will read your Keep items and show a preview. Only items marked Keep will appear in the generated manifest — all other dispositions are excluded automatically.

4
Fill in your shipment details

Complete the fields in Step 2 — your name, origin address, destination, shipping company, and container number. These details appear in the header of both documents and are required for the customs declaration.

5
Generate your documents

Tap one of the three buttons in Step 3 to download your manifest. Each format serves a different purpose:

📄 PDF Manifest

A print-ready, locked document formatted for official SAR customs submission in Honduras. This is the file you hand to the customs agent at the port of entry. It includes the full bilingual inventory, declared values, category subtotals, and a signature line. It cannot be edited after download.

📝 Word Document (DOCX)

An editable version of the same manifest that can be opened in Microsoft Word or Google Docs. Use this when your shipping company needs a file they can reference or annotate, or when you need to make a correction before submission. You can also use it to add a company letterhead or adjust formatting.

⬇ Download Both

Downloads both the PDF and the DOCX in a single click. Recommended when you are ready to submit — keep the PDF for customs and send the DOCX to your shipping company.

↺
Updating the manifest

As you continue evaluating items in the Decision Tree, your manifest will need to be regenerated. Simply re-export the JSON backup from the Decision Tree app, reload it here, and download fresh documents. The manifest will always reflect your most current Keep list.
